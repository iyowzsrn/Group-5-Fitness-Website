Description of each column featured in the Fitness Tracker dataset.

Age of the individual in years.

Gender of the individual.

Weight

Weight of the individual in kilograms.

Height

Height of the individual in meters.

Max BPM

Maximum heartbeats per minute recorded during exercise.

Avg BPM

Average heartbeats per minute during a workout session.

Resting BPM

Resting heartbeats per minute.

Session Duration

Duration of the workout session in hours.

Total calories burned during a workout session.

Fat Percentage

Percentage of body fat.

Water Intake

Water intake in liters during or after the workout.

Workout Frequency

Number of days per week the individual exercises.

Experience Level

Level of fitness experience.

BMI

Body Mass Index calculated as weight ÷ height².
