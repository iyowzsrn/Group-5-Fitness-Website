Fitness Tracker Health Analytics

A data analytics dashboard that explores fitness habits, workout performance, calorie expenditure, and health metrics using a Gym Members Exercise Tracking Dataset.

Project Overview

A web-based analytics dashboard designed to explore fitness, exercise, and health-related data. The system applies data cleaning, statistical analysis, and visualization techniques to uncover meaningful insights from fitness tracker records.

Project Objectives

General Objective

To analyze health-related data from the fitness tracker dataset using data analytics techniques in order to identify patterns, relationships, and potential health risks associated with key health indicators.

Specific Objectives

Clean the fitness tracker dataset by handling missing values, inconsistencies, and data quality issues.
Analyze the distribution and trends of key health indicators such as BMI, body fat percentage, heart rate, and physical activity levels.
Examine relationships among key health indicators and determine how they contribute to an individual's overall health status.
Provide insights and interpretations that can assist individuals in monitoring and evaluating their general health condition.
